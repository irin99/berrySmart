About Berry Smart



Farming accounts for 70% of the world’s water consumption, with many over watering their crops, while one-fifth of the world's population lacks safe drinking water. This issue primarily stems from a lack of understanding of what individual plants require in terms of water, fertilizer, sunlight and pesticides. The Berry Smart IOT device gives farmers a way to better understand their farm. A variety of sensors are strategically placed across the farm, the values of which are POSTed to a central database. We then create a dashboard for farmers that highlight these sensor values along with recommendations based off of them. For this project, we will require various sensors (soil moisture, temperature, light, humidity, pH, etc.) placed in three high priority areas in the plantation. Working as nodes in a mesh network, each node communicates the location’s soil moisture, temperature, light and pH data to a central database via the ESP32 Bluetooth and WIFI compatibility. The central databases’ data will then be analysed on a dashboard accessible to farmers. In addition, we plan on incorporating a “JoeBot” to autonomously navigate between nodes, and deliver packages such as watering plants. We are currently in the process of contacting local farmers to test our early revisions and receive user input.
